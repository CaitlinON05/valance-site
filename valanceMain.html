What we do

Everything from first numbers
to final invoice

We handle the full journey - identifying what's costing you, modelling the returns, finding available funding, and supporting delivery.

On-site Energy Audit

A thorough walk-through of your building and equipment - lighting, heating, refrigeration, HVAC, controls - with metered readings where needed. We identify every significant source of waste.

Typically half–one day on site

Savings & Payback Modelling

A bespoke financial model for your site - validated against your actual bills, with scenario comparisons across multiple measures, carbon impact, and blended payback periods.

Full written report delivered

Grant Identification

We match your site, sector, and planned measures against current UK and Irish funding schemes - BUS, SEAI, NISEP, IETF, UKSPF, Salix, and more - and advise on the strongest applications.

Included in every report

Project Oversight

Once you've approved a measure, we can manage the procurement and oversee installation - ensuring the works match the specification and the savings materialise as modelled.

The process

From scoping call to
signed-off savings

A straightforward four-step process - designed to give you clear answers quickly without months of delay.

1

Scoping Call

Free 30-minute call. We understand your site, energy spend, and priorities - and confirm whether a full audit makes sense.

2

On-site Audit

We visit your premises, inspect all energy-consuming systems, and collect the data we need. Half a day for most commercial sites.

3

Report & Model

You receive a full written report: savings by measure, payback periods, CO₂ impact, and all applicable grants - within two weeks.

4

Implement & Verify

You choose which measures to proceed with. We can oversee delivery and verify savings against the model once works are complete.
